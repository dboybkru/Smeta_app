# Design Notes

## Direction

The interface follows a quiet editorial business style inspired by https://nieuwbergen.com/.
The goal is not a landing page, but a calm estimating workspace with:

- light theme as the primary mode;
- warm neutral background, dark text, restrained green/brass accents;
- serif headings for a more premium feel;
- thin borders, low visual noise, compact controls;
- dense tables only where the user needs to compare and edit data.

## Product Rules

- Keep estimates, prices, AI, and admin areas as separate work pages.
- Do not hide operational controls behind decorative layouts.
- Use whitespace around major areas, but keep estimate rows efficient.
- Manual price markers, section markups, taxes, export, and access controls must stay visible and practical.
- Dark theme remains available, but light is the default.
